OBJECTIVE

  1. Manage human resources by implementing risk recruitment, retention, and management processes through systematic talent management.
  2. Providing quality, quality and customer-friendly service through systematic administrative and records management efficiency and prudent financial management.
  3. Plan and implement effective and effective security control through law enforcement fairly and wisely.
  4. Manage holistic human resource development to meet the aspirations and direction of the university.

BACKGROUND

Registrar Office, University Tun Hussein Onn Malaysia (UTHM) is responsible for matters relating to human resource management and facilities for University staff. Divisions and Units at the Registrar's Office are as follows:

  1. Human Resource Department
  2. Organization and Competency Management Department
  3. Security Department
